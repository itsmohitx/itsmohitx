Read this first, because some of the essays here sound darker than I am.

I am an optimist. Not the soft kind. I think the next twenty years can be the best stretch in human history. We can delete whole diseases, lift the global floor to a comfortable life, and become a species that finally leaves its planet. I believe this more than I believe almost anything.

The cautionary pieces are not doom. They are the to-do list. I write about :trail[recapture]{slug=the-recapture-pattern}, behavior capture, and :trail[the two-tier world]{slug=the-two-tier-future} for the same reason an engineer writes about load limits. Because I want the thing to hold.

A doomer says the future is dark and stops. I am saying the future is bright, and here is exactly what we have to get right to earn it. That is not pessimism. That is the optimism that actually does the work.

If you take one thing from this site, take this. I am betting on us. I just refuse to bet blindly.
